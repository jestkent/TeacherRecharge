Quick Relief Techniques

Try these evidence-based techniques when you need a moment to reset:

  • Box Breathing: Inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 4, hold for 4.
  • 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding: Identify 5 things you see, 4 things you feel, 3 things you hear, 2 things you smell, and 1 thing you taste.
  • 3-Minute Mindfulness: Focus on your breath, observe your thoughts without judgment.

Boundary Setting

Healthy boundaries are essential for preventing burnout:

  • Designate work-free times in your schedule
  • Create an after-school shutdown ritual
  • Practice saying "no" to additional responsibilities
  • Communicate your limits clearly and professionally

Setting Healthy Boundaries

Here are some templates you can adapt for your specific situation:

Email Templates

After-Hours Contact: "Thank you for your message. I check and respond to emails between [time] and [time] on school days. For urgent matters, please contact [appropriate contact]."

Additional Responsibilities: "While I appreciate you thinking of me for this opportunity, I need to decline at this time as I am focusing on [current priorities]. I'd be happy to reconsider in [timeframe] when my schedule may allow for it."

Workload Management Plan

  • Designate specific days/times for grading and lesson planning
  • Create a "must do" vs "nice to do" list each week
  • Set a departure time from school and honor it
  • Schedule at least one school-free day on weekends
